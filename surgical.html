Cysts

Fluid-filled or semi-solid sacs beneath the skin, often painless but sometimes tender or inflamed, commonly on the face, neck, or trunk. Treatments involve surgical excision, drainage, or corticosteroid injections to reduce inflammation.


Mole Removal

A procedure to remove benign or suspicious moles for cosmetic reasons or cancer concerns, typically on the face or body. Techniques vary based on the mole's nature. Treatments include shave excision, surgical excision, or laser removal for select cases.


Skin Cancer

A broad term for abnormal skin cell growth, including basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and melanoma, often caused by UV damage. Treatments depend on type and stage, ranging from surgical removal and cryotherapy to chemotherapy or immunotherapy.
